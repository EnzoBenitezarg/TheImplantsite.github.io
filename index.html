The Implant

What is The Implant?

The implant is a contraceptive method that consists of a small plastic rod, like a matchstick, that the doctor places under the skin of the upper arm. It prevents pregnancy.

How Does The Implant Work?

The implant releases a hormone (progestin) that stops ovaries from releasing eggs. It also thickens cervical mucus, blocking sperm.

Good Things and Bad Things about The Implant

Good Things (Advantages)

  • Very effective (99%)
  • Lasts for 3 years
  • No daily pills to remember

Bad Things (Disadvantages)

  • Periods might change
  • Doesn't protect from STDs
  • Needs a doctor for insertion
